# Barter economy (default path)

**Audience:** All users — **no** crypto knowledge required.

## What uCoin measures

**Contributions and trades of help** within trusted circles: scans, hosting, teaching, blueprints, bug reports — see [`specs/barter-spec.md`](../specs/barter-spec.md).

## Practical units (defaults)

| Action | Example |
| --- | ---: |
| Device scan | **10** uCoin |
| Mesh / relay hour | **50** uCoin |
| Lesson taught | **100** uCoin |

Tune per community policy in implementation.

## Trust

- Prefer **in-person** or verified first contact.
- **Disputes** are social — platform does not arbitrate barter IOUs.

## Relationship to crypto

**None required.** Crypto mode is a **separate** opt-in after intake — see [`01-how-crypto-works/README.md`](01-how-crypto-works/README.md).
