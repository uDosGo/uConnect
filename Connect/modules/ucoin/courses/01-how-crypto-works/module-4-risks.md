# Module 4 — Risks and responsibilities

## Goals

- Acknowledge **volatility**, **scams**, **tax**, and **key loss**.

## Reading

- **Volatility:** prices can move sharply.
- **Scams:** phishing, fake support, “recovery” scams — **verify** sources.
- **Tax and law:** **you** are responsible for reporting and compliance in your jurisdiction.
- **Key loss:** without backups, **no recovery** by design.

## Acknowledgment (required)

> I understand that cryptocurrency involves material risks including loss of funds, irreversible transactions, and regulatory obligations. I will not rely on uDos as financial, legal, or tax advice.

**Implementation:** record timestamp + user id + vault attestation when apps enforce this gate.
