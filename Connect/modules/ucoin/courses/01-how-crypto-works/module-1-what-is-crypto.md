# Module 1 — What is cryptocurrency?

## Goals

- Distinguish **digital barter tracking** (default uCoin) from **cryptocurrency**.
- Name at least two design goals of public networks (e.g. openness, verifiability).

## Reading

- **Cryptocurrency:** native digital assets on a **decentralised ledger** (many designs exist).
- **uCoin (barter):** a **local / mesh-synced** contribution score — **not** on-chain by default.

## Quiz

1. **In one sentence, what is the main difference between uCoin in barter mode and Bitcoin?**  
   *Sample answer:* Barter uCoin is an off-chain community ledger between trusted contacts; Bitcoin is a decentralised network asset with its own consensus rules.

2. **True or false:** Enabling crypto mode is required to use Sonic device scans for barter credits.  
   *Answer:* **False** — barter mode does not require crypto.
