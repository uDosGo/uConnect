# Course outline — How Does Crypto Work? (uCoin intake)

**Pre-v5** · **Gate** for uCoin **Phase 2** crypto bridge

## Module 1 — Money without a bank (~25 min)

- Ledgers, double-entry intuition, and why “not your keys” matters.
- Difference between **barter accounting** (uCoin default) and **on-chain** assets.

## Module 2 — Keys and custody (~30 min)

- Hot vs cold; seed phrases; why screenshots are a bad idea.
- uDos posture: **you** hold keys; uDos does **not** custody.

## Module 3 — Risk landscape (~30 min)

- Volatility, scams, phishing, fake wallets.
- Regulatory awareness (high level — not legal advice).

## Module 4 — Stellar bridge context (if enabled) (~20 min)

- Read [`modules/ucoin/specs/crypto-spec.md`](../../modules/ucoin/specs/crypto-spec.md) for the **optional** bridge model.
- When **not** to turn crypto on.

## Module 5 — Risk acknowledgment (~15 min)

- Written acknowledgment checklist (pair with vault `system/` or module CLI when implemented).
- **No** crypto mode until this module is complete.

**Total (illustrative):** ~2 hours + reading specs.
