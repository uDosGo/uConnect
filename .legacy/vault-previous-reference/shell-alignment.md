# uDOS Shell — Classic Modern Alignment

## Goal

The TUI should feel as intentional, attractive, and interactive as a GUI without pretending to be one.

## Philosophy

- mono-first
- high contrast
- compact but readable
- interactive and fun
- restrained rather than flashy

## Direction

Use terminal-native interaction patterns that feel alive:

- strong hierarchy
- clear selection states
- helpful transitions where terminal-native and lightweight
- focus on speed and delight through typography, framing, and motion cues that suit the terminal

## Stack posture

For terminal-first utility work, Bubble Tea + Lip Gloss is the preferred direction where Go is suitable.

## UX principles

- never confuse the user with surprise browser launches
- always show what happened, what failed, and what to do next
- make common flows guided and enjoyable
- keep output SSH-safe and readable
