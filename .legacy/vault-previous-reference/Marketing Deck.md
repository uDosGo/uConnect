## Short Synopsis

*Aussie Rock Rhapsody* is a heart-thumping, laugh-out-loud-crying jukebox musical set in the Australian bush during the 80s. In Act 2: Jimmy returns home after doing all the dumb things, to find the place — and himself — at a crossroads. As the homecoming B&S Ball kicks off, the stage is set for wild reunions, deep reckonings, and comedy-fuelled self-discovery. With smash hit Aussie rock music as its heartbeat, the story becomes a journey of reclamation, identity, and the sound of a country trying to find its voice.

---

## Director’s Statement

This show is a love letter to the music that raised us, the Country that shaped us, and the voices we’re still learning to hear. It celebrates loudness — the kind that’s joyful, angry, and brave.

*Aussie Rock Rhapsody* isn’t about nostalgia. It’s about reckoning with it. Through the rhythm of our shared anthems, we ask: who gets to belong? And what does home sound like when everyone sings?

This piece fuses jukebox familiarity with radically contemporary truth-telling. It’s not just a fun, musical party — it’s a reckoning for Generation X, set to a beat you know by heart.

## Featured Character Bios (Short Format)

**Jimmy** – Farm-raised and emotionally blocked, Jimmy returns home broken by grief. His journey from silence to song is the show’s emotional spine.

**Bella** – Proud and grounded, Bella is done being quiet. She sings for her people, her land, and herself — with power.

**Davo** – The drag tradie with more heart than he lets on. Comic relief turned queer icon.

**Evie** – Flashy, fierce, direct, pragmatic (if not rude), and powerful. She never gets burned and see truth in realism.

**Sister Girl** – Bella’s sibling. Sharp-tongued, fast-witted, and fiercely connected to Country.

**Kodi (Island Boy)– Heart of the ensemble. Carries cultural wisdom with warmth and unexpected weight.
