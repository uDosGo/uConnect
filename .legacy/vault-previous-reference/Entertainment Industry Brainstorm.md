### **DVE Brainstorm Summary**

#### **Key Focus Areas**
1. **Metro Markets**
   - Target **Sydney, Melbourne, Perth, SEQ** (Brisbane + GC/SC = one market).
   - Focus on CBD areas within a **10-minute walk** of central transport hubs.
   - **Controlled zones**: Operate in key metro areas; collaborate with third parties for areas outside, with third parties assuming risk.

2. **Finance and Security**
   - Explore **business loans** for acquiring commercial real estate to lease back for operations.
   - Investment opportunities in **unique properties** (e.g., theatres, hotels, destination venues).

3. **Ticketing Operations**
   - Develop scalable, adaptable, and fast processes for:
     - Concept-to-ticket sales.
     - Staffing and entertainment fulfillment.

#### **Experience Concepts**
- **Metro Experiences**: Small, vibrant city events catering to professionals and creatives.
- **Destination Experiences**: Larger, immersive events at unique venues or locations.
- **Side Hustle Empowerment**:
  - Attract “cool” self-employed individuals (artists, trainers, freelancers).
  - Enable side hustles or hobbies to support full-time income.

#### **Technology Utilization**
- **Recruitment and HR Tech**: Automated hiring, training, and payroll solutions.
- **Crowdsourced Apps**: For staffing, security, and first aid.
- **Self-Hosted Pop-Up Event Platform**: An "Uber for events" app for individuals to host events.

#### **Event & Venue Concepts**
- **Pop-Up Events**:
  - Flash raves, silent discos, themed parties.
  - Dinner party circuits (e.g., murder mysteries in mansions).
  - Progressive or synchronized smaller events.
- **Ticketed Shows**:
  - Musical theatre, opera, tribute concerts, and interactive sensory experiences.
- **Niche Social Experiences**:
  - Fitness clubs (e.g., social runs, dance fitness).
  - Personality-matched small-group gatherings.
- **Interactive Tours**:
  - Self-guided historical or cultural walking tours.
  - Escape rooms or puzzle-based events.

#### **Upsell Opportunities**
- Vending machines for physical products.
- Photo booths for instant keepsakes.
- Afterpay or lay-buy options to eliminate price barriers.

#### **Target Audiences**
- **Travelers and Locals**:
  - **18+ professionals**: Couples, singles, and groups without children.
  - Tailored experiences segmented by **age groups and preferences**.
- **Ultimate Weekender**:
  - Packages combining relaxation (spa, pool) with entertainment (theatre, brunch, tours, parties).

#### **Partnerships**
1. **Venue Partnerships**: Collaborate with underutilized spaces (theatres, ballrooms, hotels, pubs).
2. **Hospitality & Travel**:
   - Partner with **hotel brands** for themed stays and function spaces.
   - Collaborate with **airlines and transport** for bundled offerings.
3. **Media**:
   - TV/radio infomercial partnerships.
   - Owned digital media outlet for cost reduction and SEO leverage.
4. **Affiliate Programs**: Leverage Fever and other platforms for content and sales.

#### **Operational Goals**
1. **Profit Generation**:
   - Fund large musical productions and creative projects.
2. **Brand Expansion**:
   - Build metro hotspots with unique experiences.
   - Develop exclusive events to differentiate and gain attention.
3. **App Development**:
   - For customer experiences: AR/VR tours, self-guided activities.
   - For operational efficiency: talent management, risk training, and staff rostering.
4. **Customer Database**:
   - Build a 1M+ ticket-buying database leveraging Audience Republic tools.
5. **Industry Events**:
   - Host a **national conference and awards gala** with local feeder events for credibility.

#### **Strategic Positioning**
- **Amazing Experiences in Ordinary Spaces**:
  - Focus on transforming everyday spaces into extraordinary venues.
- **High-Value Niches**:
  - Avoid oversaturated markets (e.g., mainstream media, big tech).
  - Partner with industry leaders and take a small clip of value generated.

#### **Partnership Principles**
- **Long-term, mutually beneficial collaborations**.
- Partnerships with **industry leaders only**, ensuring exclusivity and high standards.
- Avoid directly competing with key partners to maintain complementary roles.

#### **Next Steps**
1. Prioritize and refine event concepts with the highest commercial potential.
2. Secure partnerships in target markets and industries.
3. Develop or source tech solutions to streamline operations and customer experiences.
4. Launch metro pilot programs to validate concepts and build momentum.
5. Build scalable systems for rapid deployment of experiences and events.
