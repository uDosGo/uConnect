# Module 5 — Stellar and the optional bridge

## Goals

- Explain why **Stellar** is listed as the **primary bridge** target (fast, low-cost operations — **informal** description).
- Understand **one-way barter → Stellar** as **opt-in** and **non-custodial**.

## Reading

- **Stellar** is a public network; assets and issuers have specific rules — read network docs.
- **uCoin bridge (spec):** moves a **declared** barter balance into a **user-controlled** Stellar workflow — **not** a promise of value or listing.

## Demo (intent)

- Create a **testnet** account and receive test **lumens**; optionally view on a testnet explorer.

## Quiz

1. **Does uDos custody your Stellar keys?**  
   *Answer:* **No.**

2. **Is the bridge one-way in the v1 spec?**  
   *Answer:* **Yes** — barter → Stellar representation per `crypto-spec`; details in implementation.
