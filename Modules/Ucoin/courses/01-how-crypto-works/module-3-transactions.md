# Module 3 — How transactions work

## Goals

- Describe **broadcast**, **confirmation**, and **fees** at a high level.
- Understand **irreversibility** once confirmed.

## Reading

- Transactions are **proposed**, **signed** with a private key, then **broadcast** to the network.
- **Validators / consensus** participants include transactions in the ledger according to network rules.
- **Fees** compensate the network; amounts vary by chain and congestion.

## Demo (intent)

- Send a **testnet** transaction on **Stellar** (tiny amount), then view it in an explorer.

## Quiz

1. **Can a confirmed transaction be “undone” by uDos support?**  
   *Answer:* **No** — networks are designed for finality; uDos does not control the chain.

2. **What do fees generally pay for?**  
   *Answer:* Network operation and inclusion / anti-spam (wording varies by chain).
