# Air-gap transfer (T5)

- **Profile:** SQLite only at `~/.udos/cells.db`; no cloud or LMDB sync.
- **Methods:** QR payloads (spatial ecosystem), USB/SD copy of vault exports, serial point-to-point.
- **Imports:** scan or copy back into vault / DB per product UX.
