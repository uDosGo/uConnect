## Tips for Writing

**Student lane:**
- Use conversational, encouraging tone
- Focus on "what you'll build" not "what you'll learn"
- Include actual commands that work
- Show expected output where helpful
- End with practical applications

**Public story lane:**
- Use present tense, active voice
- Make the Wizard the protagonist
- Personify technology (Oracle, Dungeon, etc.)
- Include sensory details (sounds, colors, textures)
- End each week with a hook for next week

**Lexicon:**
- Keep technical column precise
- Make story column vivid and imaginative
- Keep student column practical and clear
- Ensure all three columns align conceptually
